About Us

What's JTECH ?

JTECH is a dynamic technology showcase where talented students of JUW present their final year projects and innovative startup ideas built on cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Virtual Reality. The event is designed to empower women by providing them a professional platform to connect with industry experts, gain exposure, and confidently demonstrate their creativity, innovation, and technical excellence to the world.

Event Activities

Schedule that we follow

Explore More

09 AM - 10 AM

Welcome & Opening Remarks

Kick off JTECH 2026 with inspiring opening remarks from the organizing committee and special guest speakers. Get ready to explore innovation and technology!

10 AM - 11 AM

Ribbon Cutting

Celebrate the official inauguration of JTECH 2026 with the ribbon-cutting ceremony, followed by insights from industry leaders highlighting the future of tech and innovation.

10:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Qirat, National Anthem & Opening Speech

The first day of JTECH26 kicks off with Qirat, the National Anthem, and an inspiring opening speech, followed by a warm welcome note to all participants and guests.

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM

Student FYP & Project Display

Students showcase their final year projects and startup ideas at their stalls. Visitors from multiple schools and colleges explore the projects, gaining insights and inspiration.

10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Judges & Chief Guests Arrival

The day begins with the arrival of judges, chief guests, and industry experts who will evaluate the projects and provide valuable feedback to the participants.

11:00 AM - 01:00 PM

Project Evaluation & Expert Visits

Judges and software industry experts explore student projects, evaluate performance, and guide participants with insights for improvement and future opportunities.

01:00 PM - 02:00 PM

Award & Prize Distribution

Winning project ideas are honored with prizes during the award ceremony, celebrating innovation and recognizing the talent and hard work of JUW students.

02:00 PM - 02:30 PM

Closing Note & Group Pictures

JTECH26 concludes with a closing note, recognition of the core team with shields, and group photos to commemorate a successful event.
