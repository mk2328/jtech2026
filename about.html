About JTECH

About JTECH

Empowering Innovation & Future Women in Tech

JTECH is an annual flagship technology event organized by the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Jinnah University for Women. It provides a powerful platform for talented women to showcase innovative Final Year Projects and startup ideas based on emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Virtual Reality. With strong industry presence, JTECH bridges academia and industry, transforming creativity into real-world impact.

  • Showcasing Innovative Final Year Projects & Startups
  • Industry Mentorship & Professional Networking
  • Empowering Women through Technology & Innovation

“Innovation thrives where opportunity meets determination. JTECH is where ideas turn into impact.”
