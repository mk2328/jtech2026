EVENTS DETAILS

JTECH 2026 – Empowering Innovation & Women in Technology

JTECH 2026 is a dynamic technology showcase organized by Jinnah University for Women, where talented final-year students present their innovative Final Year Projects (FYPs) and startup ideas built on cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Virtual Reality, and modern software solutions.

Designed to empower women in technology, JTECH provides a professional platform for students to confidently demonstrate their creativity, problem-solving skills, and technical excellence. The event bridges the gap between academia and industry by connecting students with software companies, experts, judges, sponsors, and educational institutions.

JTECH 2026 Event Overview

JTECH 2026 spans over two impactful days, featuring project exhibitions, live demonstrations, industry evaluations, expert interactions, and award ceremonies. Students showcase real-world solutions through interactive stalls, while visitors from schools, colleges, and the tech industry explore innovative ideas and emerging technologies.

Importance of JTECH

JTECH plays a crucial role in nurturing future women leaders in technology. It offers students a rare opportunity to present their work beyond classrooms, gain real-world exposure, and receive feedback from industry professionals and academic experts.

The event promotes innovation, confidence, teamwork, and entrepreneurship. It also helps students understand industry expectations, encourages collaboration, and inspires young minds to pursue careers in technology, research, and startups.

Why Attend JTECH 2026?

JTECH 2026 is more than just an exhibition — it is a learning experience. Attendees get the chance to explore innovative projects, interact with future technologists, connect with software companies, and witness how ideas transform into impactful solutions.

Key Highlights of JTECH 2026
  • Live FYP project demonstrations and startup idea showcases
  • Project evaluation by judges, chief guests, and industry experts
  • Participation from schools, colleges, sponsors, and software companies
  • Award and prize distribution for winning project ideas
  • Recognition of JTECH core team with shields and certificates
